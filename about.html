About Me

My first name is Erica Jayne, but almost everyone calls me EJ. I am a Licensed Mental Health Counselor (LMHC) in the State of Florida (MH13199) and a National Certified Counselor (NCC) specializing in individual, family therapy, and forensic mental health services.

My journey to become a counselor began early. I discovered that I had a strong heart for social justice at a young age, a characteristic that I can most certainly attribute to my parents. Over the years I have worked in many different community mental health and community social service settings such as a Community Case Worker for Big Brothers Big Sisters; Case Worker for Catholic Charities in the Our Mother's House Project; under grants from the Untied Nations High Commission on Refugees (UNHCR); with cancer patients, nurses and medical staff at Moffitt Cancer Center, using expressive art and healing; working with sexual assault and sex-slave trafficked victims to overcome severe trauma and abuse; and addiction / treatment work as a former Program Director at a Drug and Alcohol Residential Treatment Program.

During my time in graduate school I interned with the Jail Diversion Program at the Public Defender's Office of the Sixth Judicial Circuit, located in Pinellas County. There I realized my strong passion for empowerment and holistic therapies. I developed my creative style of collaborating with clients, finding strength-based views of the present situation rather than the client remaining stuck in grief, pain, and trauma. This style allows me to incorporate the creative healing arts, NLP Hypnosis when appropriate and requested by the client, as well as traditional talk therapy methods.

Throughout all of my diverse experiences one major theme always seems to emerge; I want to know more about every person's story in order to better understand how to help them heal, grow and feel more empowered in the world!

I have continued my passion to work within my field through numerous trainings in trauma, addiction, domestic violence prevention, mindfulness, NLP Hypnosis, the Creative and Healing Arts, mitigation specialist services and forensic mental health services. I work to keep myself balanced, joyful and passionate, so that I may empower my clients to live a more holistic and abundant life.

I absolutely love my work and I feel blessed everyday to be in my field.
